Why Arc DEX

Built for programmable money

Arc's architecture removes the compromises of legacy DEXs. USDC as the gas token means no ETH needed, ever.

Sub-second finality
Transactions are irreversibly final in under one second. No waiting for multiple confirmations — your swap settles before you close the tab.
Stable, predictable fees
Gas is priced in USDC. Fees never spike during high-activity periods — you pay cents regardless of market conditions or network load.
Opt-in privacy
Confidential transactions with selective disclosure. Choose what to reveal on a per-transaction basis — compliance-ready from the start.
Stablecoin FX engine
Powered by StableFX — real-time RFQ execution with onchain settlement. Institutional-grade FX for USDC, EURC, and yield-bearing assets.
Crosschain bridging
CCTP-native bridge and Unified Balance via App Kit. Move USDC across Ethereum, Solana, and 20+ EVM chains — one click, one balance.
AI agent ready
ERC-8004 onchain identity for agents. Autonomous AI can hold wallets, place swaps, and settle value — Arc is built for the agentic economy.
How it works

Three steps to swap

01
Connect your wallet
Add Arc Testnet (Chain ID: 5042002, RPC: rpc.testnet.arc.network) to MetaMask, Rabby, or any EVM wallet. Get testnet USDC from the Circle Faucet.
02
Choose your pair
Select from USDC, EURC, and USYC. Enter the amount you want to send. The rate is calculated on-chain using StableFX RFQ — no AMM slippage on stable pairs.
03
Confirm and settle
One signature. Finality in under one second. USDC gas fees are typically fractions of a cent. Your tokens arrive before you can refresh the page.
